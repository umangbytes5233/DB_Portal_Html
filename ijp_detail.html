Position of City Chief - Ambala
Job role insights
Date of IJP Posting

26th Jan 2023

Designation

Deputy News Editor / News Editor

Preferred Education

Graduate/Post Graduate

Hiring location

Ambala

Reporting To

Editor - Haryana

Languages

Hindi & English

Experience/ Age Range

10-13 Yrs. / 30-37 Yrs

Key Responsibilities :
  • Ideation in News of the Day, Event of the Day and Event of the Month.
  • Creative and catchy headlines.
  • Converting routine stories into exclusive.
  • Strong hold on Layout/ Value Add/ Story Angle/ Data Analysis.
Key Requirements :
  • Team leading experience required.
  • Solution driven approach.
  • Possess ability to build connect internally and externally.
  • Able to handle pressure.
  • Solution driven approach.
APPLICATION PROCESS :
Step 1: To apply for an opening, ensure that you meet the following eligibility requirements
  • Should be able to read, speak and write English /Hindi
  • Should be a current, regular employee working in Print Division
  • Should have performance rating as "Meets Expectation" and above in the current position for the past one years
  • Should not have been a part of performance improvement plan for the current position
  • Should not have any disciplinary issues.
  • Should be meeting all the eligibility criteria (Qualification, Experience, Rating)
Step 2: Internal Job Application Form (has to be completed within 2weeks of the job posting)
  • Submit the application for Internal Job movement to your State/ Corporate HR head (approval of reporting manager is not required while submitting the application)
  • SPOC : Bittu Sharma, Chandigarh
  • mailto:Bittu.sharma@dbcorp.in
  • Apply within 3 days.
Step 3: Selection Process
  • Candidates who qualify - Their reporting manger will be intimated by HR.
  • They will have an initial interview with the State/Corporate HR & subsequent interviews with the Functional Manager/ Panel (as per the TA policy).
  • Notification to the candidate about status of shortlisting by HR.
  • Discussion & Issuing of Transfer letter to the selected candidate.
